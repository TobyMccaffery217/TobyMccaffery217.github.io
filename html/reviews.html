Make a positive difference!

Camp Thailand has established itself as a leading provider for trips to Thailand for young travelers; we send thousands of travel-hungry applicants with an appetite for adventure to Asia every year; our Camp Thailand testimonials offer a fantastic insight into the experiences of previous applicants for the program.

The Camp Thailand reviews completed by our previous participants of the program offer any prospective applicant for the Camp Thailand program the opportunity to gain an insight into what to expect from their forthcoming trip. Ex-participants outline recommendations for impending visits, reducing the likelihood of missing essential points of interest during your trip.
Here at Camp Thailand, we sincerely understand that while the program is an incredible experience; venturing to unfamiliar territory can be quite a daunting prospect for some of our applicants. As such, our reviews offer the opportunity for applicants who are preparing for an adventure to seek reassurance. After all, the Camp Thailand program is a once-in-a-lifetime experience in amongst an incredible country that simply is too good not to enjoy!

Each year, our Camp participants demonstrate an urge to partake in Camp Thailand reviews, having enjoyed what many consider to be a life-changing experience during their time exploring Asia. Following a Summer exploring some of the most breath-taking locations on the planet, our Campers leave Camp Thailand with a hunger to explore other countries, a wish that can be fulfilled by signing up for our diverse programs in locations such as Cambodia, America, Canada, South Africa, Maldives and the Philippines.
